About

Im Chris Ross Harris, Im a Designer and Photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky specializing in Branding and Visual Design Systems for projects like apps, games, installations and websites using technologies like Sketch, Adobe, HTML, CSS, WebGL, GLSL and JavaScript.

Im working at Leap Group Network as a Visual Designer.
I'm most known for my work with WORK Labs.

During my career, I have had the opportunity to collaborate on challenging projects for renowned brands such as White Stone Oyster Co. and L.C. King Mfg. Co..
